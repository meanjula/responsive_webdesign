Here is some personal details of Tarja Halonen

  1. Tarja Kaarina Halonen was born on 24thdecember 1943 at Helsinki, Finland
  2. She is 11th president of Finland and first lady to hold this position(2000-2012)
  3. She obtained her Master of Laws degree in 1968 specializing in criminal law
  4. In 1987, Halonen was appointed as Minister of Social Affairs and Health
  5. 1990 to 1991 she served as Minister of Justice
  6. She is popular for her interest on issues such as women's right and globalization
  7. In 2009 Forbes listed her among the 100 most powerful women in the world
